Red kimchi

Red kimchi that is the colour red

It's called red because it is red. Yummy.

Ingredients

  • Red chilli powder
  • Bunch of garlic and ginger
  • Just enough fish sauce
  • One nashi
  • Cabbage, carrots and daikon

Steps

  1. Mix everything but the veggies together in a blender
  2. Chop up all the veggies and stuff'em in a jar
  3. Let sit and ferment until yummy
